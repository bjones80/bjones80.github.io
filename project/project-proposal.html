Bucket List

The purpose of this website is to show others different traveling ideas and how to have a fun time with traveling. I had an idea to compile my bucket list and to show the rating of my already experienced adventures. I also want to have a page to plan when to go and how to save the money to go. I also want a place where people could suggest places to go and how to have the best time.

  • Home

    On this page, I would put a brief description of my travels and why my bucket list is important. I would then list a several different types of bucket List. For example, I would do one for when I am home in Wyoming and what I would do there. I would also like one for places I would like to travel in the US, Road Trips, International, Family trips, basically I would like to split my bucket list into subcategories.
